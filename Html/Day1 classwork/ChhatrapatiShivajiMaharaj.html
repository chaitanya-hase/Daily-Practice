Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Introduction

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was born on 19 February 1630 at Shivneri Fort.

He founded the Maratha Empire and is remembered for his bravery and leadership.

Achievements

Main Contributions

He established a strong navy, built many forts and promoted Hindavi Swarajya.

Interesting Facts

Shivaji Maharaj respected all religions and treated everyone equally.

Famous Quote

"Freedom is the birthright of every individual."
