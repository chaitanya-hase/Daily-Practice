Lokmanya Bal Gangadhar Tilak

Introduction

Lokmanya Bal Gangadhar Tilak was a great freedom fighter, teacher, journalist, and social reformer. He inspired the people of India to fight for independence.

Early Life

He was born on 23 July 1856 in Ratnagiri, Maharashtra. He completed his education in Mathematics and Law.

Achievements

  • Started the newspapers Kesari and Mahratta.
  • Popularized Ganesh Festival and Shivaji Jayanti.
  • Inspired the Indian freedom movement.
  • Famous slogan: "Swaraj is my birthright and I shall have it."
